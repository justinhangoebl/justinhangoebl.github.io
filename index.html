About Me

I am a Data Science Master's student at Johannes Kepler University (JKU) Linz, Austria, specializing in machine learning, graph-based algorithms, deep learning, and recommender systems, with a focus on content-based recommendation methodologies.

My academic research centers on leveraging semantic IDs for next-generation recommendation systems in synergy with generative language models, a topic I am actively exploring in my Master's thesis.

During previous internships, I developed customer-oriented full-stack applications, gaining hands-on experience in cross-functional software development and professional workplace dynamics. These roles allowed me to hone technical skills while deepening my understanding of industry culture and collaborative problem-solving.
